I operate the following APRS stations:

  • SUNSET: A Digipeater/iGate/Weather in the Outer Sunset, San Francisco, California, USA, with a 6.5 dBi gain VHF antenna at 56 MAMSL.
    This station is strategically placed to pick up all western San Francisco and near-shore Pacific Ocean APRS traffic. Because of it's clear view west to north-west, it's been known to pickup trans-Pacific balloons as well.

    Equipment:
    1. Raspberry Pi 3B (Upgraded 2016-10-19)
    2. TNC Pi
    3. Yaesu FT-50
    4. Alinco DR-1200T (Upgraded 2016-10-19)
    5. Comet GP-6 Dual-Band 6.5/9 dBi Antenna
    6. APRX
    7. Argent Data Systems ADS-WS1 Weather Station (Rev. 1)
  • SUNSET-6: A receive-only ISS iGate in the Outer Sunset, San Francisco, California, USA.
    Provided to downlink ISS APRS traffic.
    Equipment:
    1. HackRF
    2. Wide-band discone antenna
  • TACO: A receive-only iGate in San Francisco's Mission District.
    This station is based on the Oro-Net model.
