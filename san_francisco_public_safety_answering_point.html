san francisco public safety answering point

According to San Francisco's very own @sf311, the Public-Safety Answering Point (PSAP) Emergency number is 415-553-8090. The Non-Emergency number is 415-553-0123.

...and I'm only publishing this here because it's near impossible to find via Google.
